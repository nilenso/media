Hello attendees!

We are nilenso, an employee-owned, remote-friendly collective based in India and Canada. We are looking forward to Functional Conf and are excited to meet you!

In order to support you on your journey to becoming a better engineer, we are giving away some books that have had an influence on our craft. You can claim them at our sponsor booth.

Here's what some of us have to say about a few of the books we’re giving away:

The Joy of Clojure

Nid: The Joy of Clojure gave me a deeper understanding of Clojure, and the philosophy behind it. Its articulate and in-depth explanations have improved the way I approach building software well beyond the world of Clojure. The emphasis on the 'why' instead of just 'how' helped me build a functional way of thinking.

On Writing Well

Atharva: William Zinsser's classic is a poignant and passionate introduction to utilitarian writing. It reshaped the way I write and inspired me to start writing down my thoughts more. Its influence for me was more than merely improving my prose—it made me a better and more confident communicator.

The Pragmatic Programmer

Shivam: The Pragmatic Programmer has helped me become a better engineer. Its clear examples helped me build a framework to guide my thinking about developing my craft.

Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs

Sandy: SICP was my introduction to the transcendental beauty of Lisps. To me, it captures the essence of abstractions better than any other introductory book on software.

Wizard Zines by Julia Evans

Prabhanshu: Wizard Zines made me realize that you don't have to be a wizard to break down and understand the complex world of operating systems and computer networks. The quaint cartoon presentation made these topics accessible to me.

Deepa: By simply focusing on the fundamentals, Wizard Zines take the mystery out, and put the magic back in programming.


We'd love to have a chat over any of these and more at our booth. For the complete list of books we are giving away, check out our brochure. See you there!
